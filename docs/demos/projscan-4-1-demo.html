Proof Replay and review evidence

Check what actually ran.

Developers and agents can record proof-command outcomes locally, replay them against the current working tree, and hand reviewers one receipt with scope, freshness, and decision.

projscan prove --changed

Proof-first assessment

Pick the safest next fix.

projscan assess turns local quality, bug-hunt, and preflight evidence into Proof Cards. Each card names the risk, the safest change shape, and the commands that prove the risk went down.

projscan assess --mode fix-first
$ projscan assess --mode fix-first --format markdown Proof-first assessment   Fix first: maintainability hotspot src/core/bugHunt.ts combines ranking, evidence, and output.
